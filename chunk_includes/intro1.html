Instructions

Welcome! In this experiment, you'll be reading English sentences and answer one comprehension question after each sentence.

The sentences you read will be presented to you automatically, word by word, at a steady pace. It'll seem a bit fast at first, but you'll get used to it quickly. It's about as fast as people normally read words.

Before each sentence, you will see a cross ("+") to indicate that you may begin a sentence. When you see the cross, get ready to read a sentence. When you are ready to start reading, hit the spacebar and the sentence will unfold automatically.
